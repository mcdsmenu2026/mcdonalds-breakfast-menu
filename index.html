What's New on McDonald's Breakfast Menu Canada 2026

The biggest breakfast surprise in Canada for 2026 is something nobody expected — but once you taste it, it makes perfect sense. McDonald's Canada brought out the Breakfast Poutine for a limited time, and you can only get it in Atlantic Canada and Quebec.

It has crispy potato pieces made with 100% Canadian potatoes, topped with scrambled eggs, sausage, tomatoes, green chilies, and onions — then covered with cheese curds and a creamy hollandaise sauce.

It's heavy and delicious, and that's the whole idea. Canadians have wanted this for years, and McDonald's finally did it. McDonald's Canada also came out with the Smoky Maple Breakfast Collection earlier this year. It has three items: the Smoky Maple Bacon and Egg McMuffin, the Smoky Maple Sausage and Egg McGriddles, and the Smoky Maple Chicken McMuffin.

The smoky maple taste feels like a true Canadian flavor — not just a fancy name. The McGriddles version is especially good compared to the regular menu items. If you haven't tried the Smoky Maple collection yet, don't wait — these limited-time items can go away very quickly at McDonald's.

McDonald's Breakfast Classics Canada Still Worth Ordering

Some items don't need to change at all. The Egg McMuffin has been on the menu for many years, and it's still the most popular breakfast item at McDonald's Canada. At about CA$5.49 and only 290 calories, it tastes great, fills you up, and doesn't cost too much. The Sausage McMuffin with Egg is a bit bigger and more filling.

Many people order it every single morning — not because it's new or exciting, but because it's always good. These old favorites have stayed on the menu for so long because they never let you down. The Big Breakfast platter doesn't get talked about much, but it's really worth trying.

If you want a full, proper breakfast, this has it all — scrambled eggs, a sausage patty, hash browns, and a biscuit. Hotcakes are also a great choice if you want something a little sweet. Add a McCafé coffee on the side and you've got one of the best deals on the whole breakfast menu. These simple, classic items have been around for a long time — and they're not going anywhere.

Hidden Gems on McDonald's Breakfast Menu Canada

Here's what most people miss: the McDonald's breakfast wrap is genuinely one of the most filling options on the entire menu, and it rarely gets mentioned in the same breath as the McMuffin. The Sausage, Bacon and Egg Breakfast Wrap packs over 750 calories and serious protein into a soft tortilla.

It's messy in the best way, and it keeps you full well past noon. Regulars who order it rarely go back to anything else for a weekday breakfast. The other hidden gem is the Fruit and Maple Oatmeal. I know — oatmeal at McDonald's sounds like a compromise.

It's not. At 320 calories and around CA$4.99, it's one of the most nutritionally balanced items on the menu, and the combination of cream, brown sugar, and apple slices makes it genuinely satisfying.

Dietitians of Canada consistently point to whole grain oats as a strong breakfast staple, and this one actually delivers on that. If you're watching your intake but still want a hot morning meal, this is the item most people overlook.

Also worth knowing: you can ask for it without the brown sugar or cream if you want to cut it down further. Staff know the customization — you just have to ask.

McDonald's Breakfast Hours Canada 2026

Most McDonald's locations across Canada start serving breakfast at 5:00 AM. Weekday service typically runs until 10:45 AM, and on weekends most locations extend that to 11:00 AM. That difference matters more than people realize. If you're pulling up at 10:50 on a Tuesday and expecting a full breakfast menu, you may be out of luck at many locations.

One thing worth knowing: franchise-owned locations can set slightly different hours than corporate stores. Airport McDonald's locations and some 24-hour spots operate on different schedules entirely. The most reliable way to check is through the McDonald's Canada app or the store locator on mcdonalds.ca.

Don't assume — especially on holidays, when breakfast hours at some locations shift significantly. And no, McDonald's Canada does not offer all-day breakfast in 2026. Breakfast items are a morning-only window. A few McCafé drinks and bakery items stay available through the day, but the full breakfast menu wraps up before noon.

How to Order Smarter at McDonald's Breakfast Canada

The McDonald's Canada app is the single most underused tool for breakfast ordering. Beyond the CA$1 coffee deal, the app runs rotating offers on McMuffins, combo upgrades, and free item rewards through the MyMcDonald's Rewards program. Points accumulate on every order and can be redeemed for free breakfast items once you hit reward tiers. Most people who skip the app are paying full price for meals that app users are getting at a meaningful discount.

A few ordering habits worth building: ask about swap options on your combo drink. Swapping a standard coffee for a flavoured latte often costs less than CA$1 extra and makes the combo feel more complete. If you're customizing for calories, the staff at most locations handle modifications without any issue — no cheese, extra egg, no sauce — it's all standard.

The common mistake people make is assuming the menu is fixed. It isn't. McDonald's breakfast is more customizable than most people know, and a good portion of the "hidden menu" that gets shared online is just standard modifications that are available to anyone who asks.

FAQs - McDonald's Breakfast Menu

  1. What time does McDonald's serve breakfast in Canada?
    Most locations begin at 5:00 AM. Weekday service ends around 10:45 AM and weekend service runs until approximately 11:00 AM. Hours vary by location, so the app is the safest way to confirm.
  2. Does McDonald's Canada have all-day breakfast in 2026?
    No. Breakfast items are only available during morning hours. Some McCafé drinks and bakery items remain available through the day, but the full breakfast menu is not available all day.
  3. What's new on the McDonald's Canada breakfast menu in 2026?
    The standout new addition is the Breakfast Poutine, available for a limited time in Atlantic Canada and Quebec. The Smoky Maple Breakfast Collection also launched earlier in 2026, featuring three maple-inspired McMuffin and McGriddles variations.
  4. How much does a McDonald's breakfast combo cost in Canada?
    Most breakfast combos fall between CA$8 and CA$12. The McValue breakfast deal offers select items for CA$5, and the CA$1 small coffee deal is available all year through the app and in store.
  5. What is the healthiest breakfast option at McDonald's Canada?
    The Egg McMuffin at 290 calories and 17 grams of protein is consistently the best balance of nutrition and satisfaction. The Fruit and Maple Oatmeal at 320 calories is a strong choice for anyone avoiding meat or looking for something lighter.
  6. Can you customize your McDonald's breakfast order in Canada?
    Yes. Most items can be modified — removing cheese, adding extra egg, or adjusting condiments. Ask at the counter or customize through the app when ordering ahead.
  7. Is the McDonald's Canada app worth using for breakfast?
    Absolutely. The CA$1 coffee deal, rotating McMuffin discounts, and MyMcDonald's Rewards points make a real difference over time. If you're a regular breakfast customer, the app pays for itself quickly.
  8. Are there vegetarian breakfast options at McDonald's Canada?
    Yes. Hotcakes, Hash Browns, Fruit and Maple Oatmeal, and the English Muffin with Butter are all meat-free. Keep in mind that items are not certified vegetarian due to shared cooking equipment.
